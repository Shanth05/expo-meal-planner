USER GUIDE

LectureFuel

A step-by-step guide to planning your student meals, managing your pantry, and building a smart weekly shopping list.

React Native Expo Snack AsyncStorage 10 Steps
Getting Started
1

Welcome Screen

Open the app and you will land on the LectureFuel welcome screen.

  • Read the 4 feature cards — Recipe Library, Weekly Planner, Shopping List, Pantry Tracker
  • Tap "Get Started →" to enter the main app
  • Your saved data loads automatically from the last session
Recipes
2

Browse Recipes

The Recipes tab is your main library of 20 student-friendly meals.

Search
  • Type any ingredient, diet, or meal name in the search box — results update instantly
Filter
  • Tap a Meal Type chip — All, Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, Snack
  • Tap a Diet chip — All, Balanced, High Protein, Vegetarian, Vegan, Pescatarian
Sort
  • Choose from — Popular, Time, Budget, Protein, A-Z
Pagination
  • Tap Next / Previous to move between pages (4 recipes per page)
  • On mobile — pull the list down to refresh
3

View a Recipe

Tap any recipe card to open the full detail screen.

  • See cook time, protein, and budget at a glance
  • Full ingredients list with quantities and supermarket aisle
  • Step-by-step cooking instructions
  • Tap "← Back to Recipes" to return
4

Save a Recipe

There are three ways to save a recipe to your favourites:

MethodHow
Save ButtonTap "Save idea" on any recipe card — turns dark green when saved
Swipe GestureSwipe the recipe card left — green "♥ Save" background reveals and saves it
Long PressHold the card for 1 second — tap "♥ Save" from the quick action bar
Tip: Saved recipes appear in the Saved tab and stay there between sessions.
Weekly Planning
5

Add a Recipe to Your Week Plan

  • Tap any recipe card to open it
  • Scroll to the "Assign to your week" section
  • Tap a day chip — Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun
  • Chip turns dark green — recipe is now assigned to that day
  • Tap the same day again to remove the assignment
Note: Assigning a new recipe to a day that already has one will replace it automatically.
6

Manage Your Week Plan

Tap the Week Plan tab to see your full 7-day schedule.

  • Tap "View" on any day to open that recipe
  • Tap "Mark cooked" to mark the day as done
  • Tap "Undo cooked" to reverse it
  • Tap "Remove" to clear a day
  • Tap "Reset week" to clear all 7 days at once
Stats shown at the top
  • Scheduled meals, completed days, total budget points, weekly protein (g)
7

Use the Auto Shopping List

Scroll down on the Week Plan tab to see your shopping list.

  • Automatically built from all meals currently in your plan
  • Each item shows: name, aisle, quantity, and which recipe needs it
  • Items already stocked in your pantry show "In pantry" and drop to the bottom
  • Items to buy show "Buy" in green
  • Tap "Manage pantry" to go straight to the Pantry tab
Pantry & Favourites
8

Track Your Pantry

Tap the Pantry tab to manage your ingredients at home.

  • Search by ingredient name
  • Filter by All / Needed this week / Stocked
  • Tap any ingredient to toggle its status:
StatusMeaningEffect on Shopping List
Missing (red)You don't have itShows as "Buy" in shopping list
Stocked (green)You already have itRemoved from shopping list
9

View Saved Recipes

Tap the Saved tab to see all your favourited recipes.

  • Stats shown: total saved, total protein, average cook time
  • Tap any card to open the recipe and assign it to a day
  • Tap "Saved" on the card to unsave it
10

Data Saves Automatically

  • Everything is saved to AsyncStorage on your device instantly
  • Favourites, weekly plan, pantry status, and completed days all persist between sessions
  • No account or internet connection required — works fully offline
Tip: Close and reopen the app — all your data will still be there.
Quick Reference

Gesture Reference

  • Tap card → Open recipe
  • Long press card → Quick actions
  • Swipe card left → Save recipe
  • Pull down → Refresh list
  • Tap day chip → Assign to plan

Tab Navigation

  • Recipes → Browse library
  • Saved → Favourites
  • Week Plan → Planner + list
  • Pantry → Ingredient tracker

CRUD Operations

  • Create → Save / assign recipe
  • Read → Browse / view recipes
  • Update → Toggle cooked / pantry
  • Delete → Remove / reset week

Data Saved

  • Saved recipes (favourites)
  • Weekly meal plan
  • Completed days
  • Pantry stocked items
